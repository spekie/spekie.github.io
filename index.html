About me

My name is German. Online I am known as "spekie" or "spekies".

I was born in Russia, Saint Petersburg. I speak Russian natively, English, and German with proficiency.

I mostly code, write shell scripts, read science articles, read PhD dissertations, and play classical piano pieces from composers like Franz Schubert in my free time.

My interests consist of computer architecture, Linux, Unix-like operating systems, low-level programming languages, the design of computer hardware, free and open-source software, and machine learning. My other interests are philosophy, natural sciences, mathematics, linguistics, and more.

I have primarily gained experience through managing Linux and other Unix-like systems for personal projects, coding, and expanding my knowledge of computers and their hardware.
