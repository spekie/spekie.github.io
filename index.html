About me

My name is German. Online I am known as "spekie" or "spekies".

I was born in Russia, Saint Petersburg. I speak Russian natively, English, and German with proficiency.

I mostly code, write shell scripts, read science articles, read PhD dissertations, play classical piano pieces in my free time, and occasionally play some games too.

My interests consist of computer architecture, Linux, Unix-like operating systems, low-level programming languages, design of computer hardware, free and open source software, and machine learning. My other interests are philosophy, natural sciences, mathematics, linguistics, and more.

I mostly have experience managing Linux systems for my personal projects, some coding, and trying to advance my knowledge on computers and their hardware.
